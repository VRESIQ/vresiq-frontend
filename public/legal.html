Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 7, 2026

Welcome to VRESIQ. We are committed to protecting your personal data and your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our AI Resume Builder SaaS.

1. Data Collection

We collect personal information that you provide voluntarily when creating an account and building resumes:

  • Account Details: Name, email address, password, profile images.
  • Resume Content: Contact information, employment history, education, skills, projects, certifications, languages, and custom details.
  • Usage Information: Anonymized interaction logs, IP address, and cookie tokens to secure user sessions.

2. Use of Your Data

Your data is processed strictly to provide core resume-building functionalities:

  • Saving, editing, and rendering your resumes.
  • Verifying account ownership via email tokens.
  • Generating ATS scoring reviews and AI-supported text rewrites.
  • Processing secure payment transitions through Razorpay API.

3. Cloud Service Integrations & Third-Party Sharing

VRESIQ shares designated data with the following partners only to complete vital tasks:

  • Cloudinary (Free Tier): Profile pictures and document thumbnails are stored securely using Cloudinary's CDN.
  • Brevo SMTP (Free Tier): Account activation and verification emails are routed through Brevo APIs.
  • Razorpay: Order checkout parameters and receipt updates are handled securely. We do not store credit card numbers or raw billing credentials on our servers.
  • MongoDB Atlas: User records and resume document structures are hosted on MongoDB's cloud datastore.

4. Account Deletion & Right to be Forgotten

In compliance with modern privacy standards (GDPR, CCPA), you have the right to request deletion of your account and all associated resume documents at any time. When deleted, all records (including Cloudinary CDN images, Atlas DB collections, and payment history markers) are purged from active stores.

To request account deletion, contact Support: vresiq.app@gmail.com or trigger the deletion utility within the settings drawer.
